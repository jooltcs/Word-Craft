Law - Օրենք - A system of rules created and enforced by a governing authority to regulate behavior within a society.
Constitution - Սահմանադրություն - A set of fundamental principles or laws according to which a country or organization is governed.
Right - Իրավունք - A legal entitlement or freedom that is protected by law.
Obligation - Պարտավորություն - A legal or moral duty to do or not do something.
Bill - Օրենքի նախագիծ - A proposed law presented to a legislative body for approval.
Judge - Դատավոր - A public official who has the authority to hear and decide legal cases.
Court - Դատարան - A place where legal cases are heard and justice is administered.
Law enforcement - Օրենքի կիրառում - The system by which members of society obey the law, including the police and other agencies.
Legal liability - Իրավական պատասխանատվություն - Responsibility under the law for one’s actions or inactions.
Justice - Արդարադատություն - The fair and impartial treatment of all individuals under the law.
Lawyer - Փաստաբան - A professional who practices law and represents clients in legal matters.
Violation of law - Օրենքի խախտում - The act of breaking a legal rule or regulation.
Legal document - Պաշտոնական փաստաթուղթ - A formal written document that states legal obligations or rights.
Civil rights - Քաղաքացիական իրավունքներ - The rights of citizens to political and social freedom and equality.
Legal advocate - Օրենքի պաշտպան - Someone who supports or defends a legal case or cause.
Rule of law - Օրենքի գերակայություն - The principle that all people and institutions are subject to and accountable to law.
Social contract - Հասարակական պայմանագիր - An implicit agreement among members of a society to cooperate for social benefits.
Legal regulation - Իրավական կարգավորում - Rules or laws created by a government to control conduct.
Criminal legislation - Քրեական օրենսդրություն - Laws related to crime and punishment.
Civil code - Քաղաքացիական օրենսգիրք - A set of laws dealing with private rights and matters such as contracts, property, and family.
Contract - Պայմանագիր - A legal agreement between two or more parties that is enforceable by law.
Emotional distress - Հոգեբանական վնաս - Mental suffering caused by someone’s negligent or intentional actions.
Legality - Օրինականություն - The quality or state of being in accordance with the law.
Legal entity - Իրավաբանական անձ - An organization or individual that has legal rights and obligations.
Supreme Court - Վճռաբեկ դատարան - The highest judicial court in a country.
Legal precedent - Դատական նախադեպ - A previous court decision that influences future cases.
Self-defense - Ինքնապաշտպանություն - The act of defending oneself from harm using reasonable force.
Lawsuit - Դատական գործ - A legal action or claim brought to court.
Trustee - Վստահված անձ - A person who manages property or assets for the benefit of another.
Prosecutor - Պետական մեղադրող - A lawyer who represents the state and brings charges against someone accused of a crime.
Crime - Հանցագործություն - An act that violates the law and is punishable by the government.
Criminal - Հանցագործ - A person who has committed a crime.
Theft - Գողություն - The act of taking someone else’s property without permission and with the intent to keep it.
Fraud - Խարդախություն - The crime of cheating someone to gain money or advantage, often through lies or deception.
Bribery - Կաշառք - Offering, giving, or receiving something valuable (like money) to influence someone’s actions illegally.
Criminal organization - Հանցավոր խմբավորում - A group of people who work together to commit serious crimes.
Felony - Ծանր հանցագործություն - A serious crime, such as murder or robbery, usually punished by a long prison sentence.
Misdemeanor - Թեթև հանցագործություն - A less serious crime, often punishable by a fine or a short jail term.
Police - Ոստիկանություն - The official body that enforces laws, prevents crime, and maintains public order.
Arrest - Ձերբակալում - The act of being taken into custody by the police for suspected involvement in a crime.
Investigation - Քննություն - The process of gathering facts and evidence to solve a crime.
Evidence - Ապացույց - Information or objects used in court to prove whether someone is guilty or innocent.
Witnesses - Վկաներ - People who have seen or have information about a crime and can give testimony.
Verdict - Դատավճիռ - The official decision made by a jury or judge about a person’s guilt or innocence.
Imprisonment - Բանտարկություն - The punishment of being confined in prison for committing a crime.
Parole - Պայմանական ազատում - The early release of a prisoner under certain conditions before the full sentence is served.
Criminal code - Քրեական օրենսգիրք - A set of laws that define crimes and the punishments for those crimes.
Vandalism - Վանդալիզմ - The act of intentionally damaging or destroying public or private property.
Cybercrime - Կիբերհանցագործություն - Criminal activities carried out using computers or the internet, such as hacking or online fraud.
Trespassing - Ապօրինի մուտք - Entering someone’s property without permission.
Money laundering - Փողերի լվացում - Hiding the illegal origin of money by making it appear legitimate.
Conspiracy - Դավադրություն - A secret plan made by two or more people to commit a crime.
Illegal possession of firearms - Անօրինական զենքի պահում - Having guns or weapons without legal permission.
Homicide - Մարդասպանություն - The act of killing another person, whether lawful (e.g. self-defense) or unlawful (e.g. murder).
Criminal negligence - Հանցավոր անփութություն - Failing to take reasonable care, leading to harm or death, and being held legally responsible.
Extortion - Շորթում - Forcing someone to give you money or something else through threats or intimidation.
Hostage-taking - Պատանդ վերցնել - The act of capturing and holding someone to demand something in return.
Identity theft - Անձնական տվյալների գողություն - Stealing someone’s personal information to commit fraud or other crimes.
Hooliganism - Խուլիգանություն - Disruptive, violent, or criminal behavior in public, often linked to groups or sporting events.
Alibi - Ալիբի - Proof that someone was elsewhere when a crime occurred and thus could not have committed it.
Prison break - Փախուստ բանտից - Escaping from a prison facility illegally.
Defendant - Մեղադրյալ - The person accused of a crime in a court of law.
Defense attorney - Դատապաշտպան - A lawyer who defends the accused in court.
Government - Կառավարություն - The organization or group of people with the authority to govern a country or state.
State - Պետություն - An organized political community under one government.
Authority - Իշխանություն - The legal power or right to give orders and make decisions.
President - Նախագահ - The head of state or government, depending on the country’s system.
Prime Minister - Վարչապետ - The head of the government in parliamentary systems.
Parliament - Խորհրդարան - A legislative body that makes or passes laws.
Government agency - Կառավարական մարմին - A specialized unit that performs a specific government function.
Legislative body - Օրենսդիր մարմին - A group of elected people who create laws.
Executive power - Գործադիր իշխանություն - The branch that enforces and administers laws.
Judicial power - Դատական իշխանություն - The branch that interprets laws and administers justice.
Politics - Քաղաքականություն - Activities related to governance, policies, and power.
Minister - Նախարար - A government official in charge of a specific department.
Economic policy - Տնտեսական քաղաքականություն - A government’s plan for managing the economy through taxation, spending, interest rates, and labor regulations.
Human rights - Մարդու իրավունքներ - Basic rights and freedoms that belong to every person, such as freedom of speech, equality, and protection from abuse.
Tariffs - Մաքսատուրքեր - Taxes imposed on imported or exported goods, usually to protect domestic industries or raise government revenue.
State budget - Պետական բյուջե - A financial plan showing a government’s expected income and expenses over a specific period.
Social security - Սոցիալական ապահովություն - Government programs that provide financial assistance to people in need, such as the elderly, unemployed, or disabled.
Official decision - Պաշտոնական որոշում - A formal choice or judgment made by a person or body in a position of authority.
Governor - Նահանգապետ - An official who governs a state or province, often appointed or elected to oversee regional administration.
Central authority - Կենտրոնական իշխանություն - The main governing body that holds power at the national level, usually overseeing the entire country.
Local government - Տեղական ինքնակառավարում - Government bodies that manage local areas such as cities or towns, handling community services and regulations.
Civil service - Քաղաքացիական ծառայություն - A body of government employees who carry out the daily work of public administration, not including elected officials or military personnel.
System of governance - Կառավարման համակարգ - The structure and processes through which a country or organization is ruled or managed.
Branches of power - Իշխանության ճյուղեր - The division of government into separate parts, typically legislative, executive, and judicial, to prevent abuse of power.
Legislative body - Օրենսդրական մարմին - A group of elected representatives responsible for making and passing laws.
Presidential system - Նախագահական ռեժիմ - A form of government where the president is the head of state and government, separate from the legislative branch.
Parliamentary system - Խորհրդարանական համակարգ - A system of governance where the executive derives its legitimacy from the legislature and is accountable to it.
Diplomacy - Դիվանագիտություն - The practice of managing international relations through negotiation and dialogue between countries.
Political party - Քաղաքական կուսակցություն - An organized group of people with shared beliefs and goals that seeks to gain and exercise political power.
State supervision - Պետական հսկողություն - Oversight and regulation conducted by government agencies to ensure compliance with laws and standards.
Electoral commission - Ընտրական հանձնաժողով - An official body responsible for organizing and monitoring elections to ensure they are fair and legal.
Referendum - Հանրաքվե - A direct vote by the public on a specific political or legislative issue.
Public order - Պետական կարգ - The general peace and security maintained by law enforcement and legal systems in a society.
